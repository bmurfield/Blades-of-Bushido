Instructions

Player Controls:

Player 1: Samurai Mack

  • A - Move Left
  • S - Move Right
  • W - Jump
  • Spacebar - Attack

Player 2: Kenji

  • Left Arrow - Move Left
  • Right Arrow - Move Right
  • Up Arrow - Jump
  • Down Arrow - Attack

Game Rules:

  • The game ends when one player loses all their health.
  • The game also ends when the timer runs out.
